Welcome to Pember's Angels

This website offers a service for pet owners who seek a compassionate, in-home euthanasia option for their beloved pets.

Our Goals

At Pember's Angels, we understand how difficult it is to say goodbye to a beloved pet. Our in-home euthanasia services are designed to provide a peaceful and compassionate farewell in the comfort of your own home. We believe in making this moment as comfortable as possible for your pets, allowing them to be surrounded by familiar sights, smells, and most importantly, their loving family.

Our experienced and empathetic team will guide you through the process and answer any questions you may have. We'll be there supporting you every step of the way and ensuring your pet's comfort and dignity are maintained throughout this process.

About the Owner, Dr. Pember

I am a recent graduate of Long Island University College of Veterinary Medicine. My experience in veterinary medicine focuses on emergency medicine in both specialty and urgent care settings where I am currently employed full time. I am no stranger to patients with poor long-term prognoses and was inspired to help owners find peaceful transitions at home. When I am not on the clock, I spend my free time with my orange feline companion, Roux.

Service Areas

We proudly serve the Westchester area, but we have extended our services to certain areas of the surrounding counties, including parts of Rockland, Putnam, and Orange. We are currently in the process of getting licenses for Connecticut.
